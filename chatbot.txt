Heart Attack
Heart attack, occurs when blood flow decreases or stops in the coronary artery of the heart, 
causing damage to the heart muscle The most common symptom is chest pain or discomfort 
which may travel into the shoulder, arm, back, neck or jaw

signs of heart attack

Discomfort in the chest, especially the center, that lasts more than a few minutes or comes and goes The discomfort may feel like heavines, fullness, squeezing, or pain
Discomfort in the upper body parts such as the arms, back, neck, jaw, or stomach This may feel like pain or general discomfort
Shortness of breath This may come with or without chest discomfort

Having Heart Attack

if you feel symtoms of heart attack do this immediately: 
1. Call AMBULANCE immediately
2. Take ASPIRIN of normal dose
3. Take Deep Breath and Coughing Deeply.


Cancer
Cancer Cancer is a group of diseases involving abnormal cell growth with the potential to invade or spread to other parts of the body These contrast with benign tumors, which do not spread


1 ->Signs and Symptoms

Possible signs and symptoms include a lump, abnormal bleeding, prolonged cough, unexplained weight loss, and a change in bowel movements 

2 -> Treatment

Surgery: The doctor removes the tissue with cancer cells
Chemotherepy: Use special medicines to kill cancer cells
Radiation Therepy: Use high radiation to kill cancer cells

3 -> Causes

Some common causes are family history, smoking, bad diet, environmental exposure and working with toxic chemicals.

Snake 
A snakebite is an injury caused by the bite of a snake, especially a venomous snake A common sign of a bite from a venomous snake is the presence of two puncture wounds from the animal's fangs

1 -> Treatment

Call ambulance [108,911]
Wash the bite with soap and water
Take antivenom as fast as Possible

2 -> Dont's 

Do not suck out the venom
Do not apply ice or immerse the wound in water totally
Do not take pain killers
NEVER handle a venomous snake, not even a dead one or its decapitated head.
